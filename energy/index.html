Project Development Experience

Patrick has worked with over 30 developers and IPPs across North America to advance nearly 2GW of installed clean energy capacity.

  • United States: 39 projects in ME, NH, VT, NY, PA, WV, VA, MD, OH, and CA.
  • Canada: 76 projects in BC, AB, SK, NS, PEI, and NB.

Patrick helps developers assess feasibility of projects, model development scenarios, acquire regulatory approvals, de-risk projects, and prepare project proposals.

He has experience in utility-scale wind, solar, storage, hydro, geothermal, natural gas, transmission, and distributed energy resource development.

Commercial Experience

Patrick has extensive market knowledge of the clean energy industry in Canada, as a consultant and, recenlty, in a procurement role at BC Hydro:
  • Supported bidders to develop proposals for the 2024 BC Hydro Call for Power.
  • Developed commercial terms for the 2025 BC Hydro Call for Power, with BC Hydro staff.

Anaytical Capabilities

Patrick has academic training and extensive professional experience in statistical modeling and data analysis. He has capabilities in the following energy modeling platforms:

  • Techno-economic models: SAM, PVWatts, GETEM, reV.
  • System planning models: COPPER.
  • Capacity expansion models: ReEDS 2.0, Calliope.
